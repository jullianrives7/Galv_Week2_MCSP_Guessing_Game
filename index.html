Jullian Rives: Guessing Game Project

Try to Guess the Secret Number!

How To Play:

  • 1. Click the Start Game Button to begin playing.

  • 2. Enter your name in the prompt and click "ok".

  • 3. A secret number is generated between 0 and 10... take your chances and guess what it is! Enter your guess in the prompt and click "ok".

  • 4. Once you have guessed correctly, your best score (lowest attempt count) will be saved and reported to you.

  • 5. If you want to play again, type "yes" into the prompt & click "ok" to try and beat your high score! If you do not want to play again, type "no" into the prompt and click "ok" or cancel out of the prompt window.

  • Thanks for playing and happy gaming!
